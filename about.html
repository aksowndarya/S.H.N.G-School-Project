Who We Are ?

S.H.N. GIRLS school is a Government Aided girls' school located in Sivakasi, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded in 1975 and is affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Board of Secondary Education. The school offers classes from kindergarten to grade 12. S.H.N. GIRLS school is known for its strong academic program and its commitment to empowering girls to reach their full potential. Mission

M

  • To provide a high-quality education that empowers young women to achieve their full potential, embrace lifelong learning, and make a positive impact on the world.
  • To provide a challenging and engaging academic curriculum that fosters intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.
  • To create a supportive and inclusive learning environment that promotes respect, diversity, and collaboration.
  • To nurture the development of the whole person, including academic excellence, character development, and social responsibility.
  • To prepare students for success in higher education, careers, and civic engagement.

History

H

S.H.N. Girls Higher Secondary School was founded in 1965 by a group of visionary individuals who were committed to providing quality education to girls in Sivakasi. The school's graduates include doctors, lawyers, engineers, educators, and business leaders. Today, S.H.N. Girls Higher Secondary School remains committed to its founding principles of academic excellence, character development, and social responsibility.
