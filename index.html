About Us




Welcome to Our S.H.N.G School !

Who We Are ?

S.H.N. GIRLS school is a Government Aided girls' school located in Sivakasi, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded in 1975 and is affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Board of Secondary Education. The school offers classes from kindergarten to grade 12. S.H.N. GIRLS school is known for its strong academic program and its commitment to empowering girls to reach their full potential. Where curiosity meets knowledge, and every student is a star in their unique constellation of learning. Explore, dream, achieve – welcome to a journey of education that goes beyond the ordinary!
